Contact

Start a conversation with Stonebranch.

If you’re interested in automation, systems, or potential collaboration with one of our ventures, this is where it starts. No pressure, no sales script — just a straightforward conversation about where you’re at and what might help.

We’re especially interested in small and veteran-owned businesses, but we’re happy to hear from any owner who cares about clean operations and long-term thinking.

Prefer not to use your default email app? You can also copy the address: contact@stonebranchcapital.com and send a message with the details below.

What to include
  • Your name & business name
  • Your location / service area
  • What you currently offer
  • Where things feel messy or manual
  • What you’d love to stop doing yourself

You don’t need a polished plan. A rough description of your reality is enough — we’ll help connect the dots between where you are and where systems or automation might actually help.
